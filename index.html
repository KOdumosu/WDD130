Lagos City

Lagos City is the most populous city in Nigeria, with an estimated population of 21 million in 2015. The estimated population for Lagos was more than 26 million in 2023 and around 30 million for the Lagos metropolitan area. Lagos is the most populous urban area in Africa. Lagos is a major African financial centre and is the economic hub of Lagos State and Nigeria at large. She is also among the top ten of the world's fastest-growing cities and urban areas having the fourth-highest GDP in Africa and houses one of the largest and busiest seaports on the continent.The Lagos metropolitan area is a major educational and cultural centre in Sub Saharan Africa. Due to the large urban population and port traffic volumes, Lagos is classified as a Medium-Port Mega city.
